Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a very tasty dish but takes quite a long time to make.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • White sauce
  • Spinach
  • Chicken mince
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Boil and dry pasta sheets.
  2. Cook chicken mince and spinach in tomato sauce and herbs.
  3. Place a layer of pasta sheet, add chicken mince mixture, and a ladleful of white sauce, and repeat till there are 6-8 layers.
  4. Place in the oven and bake till golden.
  5. Wait a day before consuming for maximum flavour.
