Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Best chocolate chip cookies according to allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 mins, or until edges are browned.
