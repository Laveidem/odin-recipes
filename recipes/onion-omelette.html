Onion Omelette

Description

Dracula's favourite breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 big onion (any kind)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • water
  • oil

Steps

  1. Chop onions and saute them in oil till they are soft.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Add a little water and let it cook, and when it dries up, add more water and repeat, till onions are caramelized well.
  4. Beat the eggs and add the mixture into the onions. Mix and fold till the eggs are cooked just enough.
